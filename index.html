Ek Pal Ka Khwaab

Thanda tha mausam, halki thi baarish,

Dil mein thi khamoshi, aankhon mein khwahish.

Uske haathon ka tha jo ehsaas,

Lagta tha jaise ruk gaya ho har saans.

Chand kadam saath chalte gaye,

Bin kuch kahe sab keh gaye.

Na thi zarurat kisi alfaaz ki,

Har lamha tha khud mein raaz ki.

Uski baaton mein tha narm sa geet,

Jaise dard bhi mehka ho kisi meet ki preet.

Nazaron ne kaha tha jo lab keh na sake,

Waqt bhi ruk gaya tha, us pal ke liye.

Phir hawaon ne ek kahani likhi,

Uski muskurahat ne khushboo bikheri.

Har pal tha jaise koi geet adhura,
